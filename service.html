ARIIMS Trading Platform

AbleAce Raakin Integrated Islamic Murabaha System (ARIIMS) is AbleAce Raakin’s specialized online solution designed for seamless Commodity Murabahah transactions. Engineered to facilitate high-efficiency executions for our corporate clients, its operational architecture and secure framework were built alongside governance guidance from the Central Bank of Malaysia. The platform is fully vetted, verified, and certified by the prominent Amanie Shariah Supervisory Board.
