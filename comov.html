Commodity Murabahah

Shariah-Compliant Commodity Trading

Murabahah means sales. Commodity Murabahah refers to the sales transaction of commodities between a seller and buyer based on an agreed cost-plus margin before the transaction takes place.

This structure ensures that the price paid is fixed, transparent, and mutually agreed upon. No interest cost is involved, providing greater security and certainty for all parties.

In today’s Islamic financial environment, Commodity Murabahah is widely used to facilitate various Shariah-compliant financial products for organisations and the public.
