Milestones

Our Story, Year by Year

From our founding to becoming a multi-award-winning global platform, every step has been guided by purpose, principle, and innovation.

  • 2006
    Establishment

    Established as an answer to the Malaysian Government's call to develop the nation as a leader in the Islamic finance industry. Our inception catalysed Malaysia as an Islamic financial hub for the Commodity Murabahah market — and we were the first in Malaysia to use palm oil and its downstream products as the base asset for this programme.

  • 2007
    First World Commodity Murabahah Trade

    Pioneered the world's first Commodity Murabahah trade using agro-based products — introducing Palm and Rubber as core assets for Shariah-compliant transactions, expanding possibilities for sustainable and ethical global trading.

  • 2010
    Transitioning to Web-Based System

    Transitioned from manual to a fully web-based trading system, revolutionising how Commodity Murabahah transactions were conducted. This shift delivered real-time trade execution, greater transparency, and a significantly improved client experience.

  • 2011
    First Middle East Client

    Marked our entry into one of the most prominent regions for Islamic finance by onboarding our first Middle Eastern client — solidifying our presence in the global market and demonstrating our ability to serve diverse financial institutions under Shariah-compliant principles.

  • 2014
    World's Single Largest Sukuk Issuance

    Participated in the world's single largest Sukuk issuance — a landmark event that showcased our capability to support large-scale, Shariah-compliant financial transactions on a global stage, reinforcing our role as a trusted partner in complex Islamic finance structures.

  • 2016 – 2025
    Awards & Industry Recognition

    A period of sustained excellence, recognised by leading global bodies in Islamic finance and banking:

    2016 — Market Leadership · Global Islamic Finance Awards
    2017 — Best Islamic Commodity Trading Company · Global Banking and Finance Review
    2017 — Best Shariah Compliant Commodity Broker · Global Islamic Finance Awards
    2019 — Most Outstanding Islamic Commodity Platform · Kuala Lumpur Islamic Finance Forum
    2020 — Excellence Award Islamic Commodity Platform · Global Islamic Finance Awards
    2025 — Excellence Award (Shari'a Compliant Commodity Brokerage) · Global Islamic Finance Awards
  • Today & Beyond
    Global Expansion & ARIIMS Evolution

    Capitalising on our ARIIMS platform, we continue expanding our global footprint into Africa, the Middle Eastern, and parts of Eastern Europe. As our business grows, ARIIMS has continuously evolved from its initial version to the latest ARIIMS Version 3 Modules, delivering enhanced functionality, greater operational efficiency, and improved support for the evolving needs of Islamic financial institutions. We are also continuously exploring new product offerings, including soft commodities and other tangible assets, to better serve our growing global client base.

  • Future
    Continuous Innovation

    Looking ahead, we remain committed to continuously enhancing the ARIIMS platform through the development of future modules and digital innovations. Our technology roadmap focuses on improving system capabilities, operational efficiency, security, and scalability while supporting the evolving needs of Islamic finance and global commodity trading. Through continuous innovation, we aim to deliver even greater value to our clients and strengthen our position as a trusted global technology and commodity trading partner.
