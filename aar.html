ARIIMS Trading Platform

Our Trading platform, ARIIMS (Ableace Raakin Integrated Islamic Murabaha System) is an innovative web-based solutions designed to facilitate Shariah-compliant commodity trading for commodity murabaha requirements. ARIIMS enables seamless, transparent, and secure trading by adhering strictly to Islamic finance principles.

Through ARIIMS, clients can effortlessly participate in commodity trading, with complete confidence. Our platform offers real-time access to a diverse range of commodities, ensuring consistent availability and a clear, fixed cost-plus margin structure with no hidden charges. By leveraging the power of technology, ARIIMS simplifies the process of managing, trading, and tracking your commodity investments in a compliant and efficient manner.

Ableace Raakin’s commitment in providing secured and Shariah-adherent trading solutions guarantees certainty and peace of mind for all clients.
