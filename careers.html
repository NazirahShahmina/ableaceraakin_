We strive to create an environment of diversity and inclusivity, where our people can thrive in a culture irrespective of ethnicity, background and approach. It's what makes AbleAce Raakin truly unique.

What are we looking for?

AbleAce Raakin is always looking for talented and motivated individuals who are passionate about Shariah-compliant finance, commodity trading, and sustainable economic growth. We prioritize candidates with a strong analytical mindset, commitment to integrity, and an outgoing personality.

Tips Before You Apply

Learn how to stand out from the rest.

Step 01

Resume

Keep your resume tailored to the role. Highlight relevant skills and use action verbs to demonstrate your achievements.

Step 02

Research

Understand our mission and values. Review the job description carefully to see how you align with our culture.

Step 03

Be Yourself

Don't be afraid to show your passion. We value individuality and want to see the real you behind the qualifications.

Step 04

Be Prepared

Research the company and practice common questions. Confidence during the interview comes from being well-prepared.
