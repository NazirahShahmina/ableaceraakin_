ARIIMS Trading Platform

AbleAce Raakin Integrated Islamic Murabaha System (ARIIMS) is AbleAce Raakin’s online solution for the convenience of Commodity Murabahah transactions for the convenience of AbleAce Raakin's Clients to conduct commodity Murabahah transactions. Its architecture, design and security were developed with guidance by the Central Bank of Malaysia. The Platform was fully vetted and certified by Amanie Shariah Supervisory Board in 2009.

Secure

With various securities features implemented on Back-End of ARIIMS trading Platform such as SFTP and STP Protocols, we ensure our platform is secured and resilient to protect client data.

Authentic

AbleAce Raakin ensures authenticity in every transaction, processing more than 200,000 transactions annually with full Shariah compliance and zero breach tolerance.

Convenient

ARIIMS is accessible via any web browser and supports interface customization with multiple themes for a personalised client experience.
