Who We Are

Our Vision & Mission

Guided by purpose — everything we do is driven by a clear vision, a focused mission, and values that define us.

Vision

To achieve the profit target set by serving every Islamic financial establishment worldwide, fulfilling their Murabahah requirement with confidence, trust, and transparency.

Mission

Leading the way in providing benefit to our customers and stakeholders through excellence in business practices — setting the standard for ethical, transparent, and Shariah-compliant service delivery.

What Drives Us

Our Five Core Values

There is more to business than just profit — how we do business is equally important. These five principles are what make AbleAce Raakin unique and guide every decision we make.

1
Trust & Integrity

We value our customers' trust as the foundation of our long-lasting relationships. This support reflects our deep integrity, holding ourselves to the highest ethical standards in every transaction.

2
Innovative

In a fast-paced environment, being innovative is crucial to our success. We continuously seek smarter ways to serve our clients and evolve our platform.

3
Clarity

Clarity and transparency are practised in everything we do, ensuring a high level of confidence and trust from our clients at every step.

4
Virtue

We believe in doing the right thing — upholding moral principles in every transaction, partnership, and decision we make as a company.

5
Teamwork

Teamwork is the key to our success. Collaboration, mutual respect, and shared purpose unite our people to deliver the best for our clients.
